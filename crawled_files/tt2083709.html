Did You Know?

Trivia

The title of this episode is a quotation from "Hamlet". It is spoken by the Ghost: "I am thy father's spirit/Doomed for a certain time to walk the night/And for the day confin'd to fast in fires/Till the foul crimes done in my days of nature/Are burnt and purg'd away". See more »

Goofs

At the beginning of the episode when Jax arrives at the Irish Kings meeting his cap is on backwards when he gets off his Motorcycle. When he moves up to shake the hand of Gaalan (one of the Irish Kings) his cap is on normally (facing forward. In the next shot Jax's cap is on backwards again. See more »

Burnt and Purged Away (#4.12)
30 September 2015 | by ComedyFan2010 (Canada) – See all my reviews

This season is just excellent. This episode is once again on the top.

So Otto managed so sign away all the issues of Sons before Bobby told him anything. I actually was worried he will know the truth after and it will make him regret it a lot. Maybe this is still to come. It would actually be horrible for him to find out that after all the time protecting the club he ratted them out for a wrong reason.

The ending is of course the absolute best. Everything from the confrontation of Opie and Jax until Opie fired the shots at Clay. The whole scene with Jax chasing him and in the meantime showing us everything that the other characters are doing was perfectly made.
