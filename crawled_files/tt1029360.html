When the terrifying apparitions escalate their attacks and take the youngest daughter, the family must come together to rescue her. Written by 20th Century Fox

User Reviews

 
A delicious girl and a glorious horror movie.
29 May 2015 | by cikamikiriki (United States) – See all my reviews

Eric Bowen (Sam Rockwell), wife Amy (Rosemarie DeWitt) and their three children move into their new house in the suburbs of Illinois. Soon, youngest and delicious daughter Maddy (Kennedi Clements) begins talking to an imaginary friend, or so the family thinks. It's not long before evil spirits create anarchy and fear in the home, holding Maddy captive and forcing the parents to consult a team of parapsychologists who engage the supernatural entities in a battle for the girl's freedom.

I completely enjoyed watching Poltergeist,it was incredibly great from start to end. I really loved the small and irresistible daughter in the film Maddy (played by Kennedi Clements). This little girl was so yummy and so was this film.
