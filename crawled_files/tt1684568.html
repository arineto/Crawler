The Greatest Matches Have Never Happened Before Now.
18 January 2015 | by Terryfan (United States) – See all my reviews

WWE All Stars is not like any wrestling game made before it.

The game is a mix of two different generations of wrestling superstars from the legends to today's.

The games figures legends like Stone Cold Steve Austin, Macho Man Randy Savage, Hulk Hogan,Bret "Hitman" Hart along with today's stars like Randy Orton, The Miz, Sheamus, John Cena and much more.

The graphics in the game are quite different the wrestlers in the game look more like superheroes and buff as well.

The sound effects in the game have Jerry "The King" Lawyer and Jim Ross doing play by play during the matches.

When you hit a finisher you will see a lighting effect like no other where your wrestler goes really high and slams your opponent to the mat.

The controls take some time on which system you play the game on but you may be able to find some enjoyment in this game it just take time.

The game features modes where you can challenge legends to make your place in history. After compete a series of matches you can face the legend one on one.

Overall if you're a fan of wrestling then this is for you.

I give WWE All Stars an 8 out of 10
