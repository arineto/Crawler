Storyline

When Spence and Hogan graduate from college, life is bleak. They have to work for heinous divorce lawyers that torture them. Spence has a girlfriend from hell and Hogan just wants to start his life already. As luck would have it, our two young men are presented with an opportunity, they develop a club of young men devoted to the older woman, the "Cougar" if you will. It is this club that ultimately guides our two heroes into young adulthood. Written by Anonymous

Perfect Plot - Great Cast
10 November 2007 | by macjense (NYC) – See all my reviews

The movie has the perfect plot and timing.

They portray the pure aspect of what a "cougar" is through the cast as well as the acting.

Its definitely a cheesy comedy, but that is what the audience is looking for, at least what I was looking for.

Its essentially a national lampoons style movie - would compare to a Van Wilder or Old School style movie.

I am looking forward to the full release to see it again.

Good quality of writing as well as producing and looks like it was done with a much larger budget than what is estimated. Always good to see this in a independent movie.
