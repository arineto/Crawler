Storyline

London's Newgate Prison of the 1750s was the atmospheric setting for this bawdy and boisterous series of 60-minute tales, the first four of which were run as part of Rediffusion's 'Playhouse' series. The stories featured historic antihero inmates such as Jonathan Wild, highway woman Jane Rawley, Lady Sarah Bellasize and Jack Sheppard. Written by Anonymous
