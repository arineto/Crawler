Did You Know?

Trivia

Artie Lange's cameo appearance in the "Goodnight Saigon" sketch was not planned. He was backstage visiting when producer Marci Klein demanded that he make an appearance and pulled him onto stage as the sketch was already in progress. This explains why he is the only one without an instrument. See more »
