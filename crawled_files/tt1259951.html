Storyline

Prefect Pilate worries why his mighty colleague, the governor of great Syria, is about to visit him. Jesus keeps preaching, not only to his disciples during the Passover preparations and Last Supper itself, but also in public, against the established order. Caiaphas convinces the Temple council such a revolutionary is too great a danger: Jesus must be arrested, tried and if unrepentant handed to the Romans for execution. Reluctant informer Judas is pressed to betray his master, who just foretold this and his sacrifice, by a kiss. Written by KGF Vissers
